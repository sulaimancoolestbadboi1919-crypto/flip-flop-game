About The Game

FlipFlop is a professionally designed memory challenge game that sharpens your cognitive abilities. Test your memory skills by finding matching pairs across three difficulty levels. Track your progress, beat your personal records, and compete for the highest scores.

🎯

How To Play

1

Start a Game

Click the PLAY button on the main menu to begin a new game session

2

Choose Difficulty

Select your preferred difficulty level: Easy (16 cards), Medium (20 cards), or Hard (30 cards)

3

Flip Cards

Click on any card to flip it and reveal the hidden emoji. Try to memorize the positions as you go

4

Match Pairs

Find all matching pairs by flipping two cards with the same emoji. Successfully matched pairs stay revealed

5

Maximize Your Score

Complete the level with the fewest moves and lowest time to achieve the best score and unlock achievements
